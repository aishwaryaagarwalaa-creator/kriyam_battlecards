Why Status Quo ALWAYS Loses to Kriyam (Post April 2026)
IRDAI FMF Clause 4 — Board FMC mandateEvery insurer must have a board-approved Fraud Monitoring Committee with a nominated Nodal Officer and quarterly board reporting. Excel cannot produce a board-approved FMR. This is a legal mandate, not a suggestion.
IRDAI FMF Clause 6 — RFI SystemQuantitative + qualitative Red Flag Indicators required at both underwriting AND claims stage. WhatsApp flags are not an RFI system. The mandate requires a documented, auditable system.
IRDAI FMF Clause 7 — IIB ReportingConfirmed fraud cases must be reported to Insurance Information Bureau within 7 days in mandated format. Excel cannot auto-generate IIB submissions. Manual process cannot meet the 7-day deadline at scale.
IRDAI FMF Clause 8 — Field InvestigationStructured investigation workflows with documented evidence, chain of custody, mandatory photo/video, court-admissible. WhatsApp photo sends do not constitute court-admissible evidence.
